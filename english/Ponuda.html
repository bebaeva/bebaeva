Gold package for a pregnant woman

The new project of our club reads:
"In meeting the new addition it's needed the whole family"
Working with a pregnant woman individually and with her choice of close members.
We called the project: “Gold package for a pregnant woman“
It consists of:

1. 4 DVD-a „A pregnancy course in your home“- collection
2. Theoretical knowledge and exercises for childbirth are done in the "Beba Eva" club by appointment
3. For complete safety, 15 days before birth, the mother receives a Doppler to listen to her baby's heartbeat in her womb at home.
4. Start the birth with us together in your home.
5. Childbirth will be natural, painless, without cutting the perineum and takes about 3 to 5 hours
6. With our education and practice, we guarantee safe and easy breastfeeding
7. Home visits after childbirth up to 2 months of age (available 24 hours)

Practical exercises on how to wrap, bathe and care for a newborn's navel

After giving birth in home care for newborns, various bacterial infections of the umbilical cord often occur, due to which the newborn has to go back to the hospital to the children's ward for antibiotic therapy after a few days at home. The risk of umbilical cord infection is possible even 2 months after birth and therefore you need to know how to nurture the umbilical cord and prevent infection.

In our and your club of pregnant women "Beba Eva" we have recorded DVDs on this topic, as well as in the club Beba Eva educational course.
